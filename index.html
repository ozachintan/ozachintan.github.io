About

Adv. Chintan Oza

Advocate · Registered Patent Agent · Patent and Trade Mark Attorney

Bridging the technical world of innovation and the rigorous demands of the courtroom.

I am an Advocate, Registered Patent Agent, and Patent and Trade Mark Attorney based in Silvassa. My practice is built on a rare duality — the analytical precision of an IT Engineer and the persuasive advocacy of a seasoned litigator. I began my career in the technology sector, where I developed a logic-first approach to problem solving. Today, I apply that same engineering mindset to the law, identifying nuances in every case that others might overlook.

My work rests on two pillars: courtroom advocacy and intellectual property excellence. I translate technical complexity into compelling legal arguments, representing clients with grit and strategy across civil, criminal, and IP matters. I help innovators and businesses secure their trade marks and patents, delivering a global standard of protection right here in Dadra and Nagar Haveli.

Beyond the law, I am an avid long-distance driver, a singer, and a writer of poetry and professional write-ups — pursuits that sharpen the focus, patience, and precision I bring to every brief and argument. I do not just offer legal services; I offer a strategic partnership grounded in transparency, expertise, and a commitment to life-long trust.

Deed Drafting & Registration

Professional drafting and registration of all conveyancing instruments and legal documents.

Sale Deeds
Transfer of immovable property with full title guarantee
Lease Deeds
Long-term and short-term lease agreements for all property types

"Every transaction deserves a document that protects your rights today and withstands scrutiny tomorrow."

Adv. Chintan Oza drafts all conveyancing instruments with meticulous attention to legal accuracy, stamp duty compliance, and registration requirements under the Registration Act, 1908 and the Transfer of Property Act, 1882.

Clients receive documents that are not merely legally valid, but strategically worded to anticipate and pre-empt future disputes, backed by the clarity that comes from combining legal training with a technical engineering mindset.

We handle drafting, vetting, notarisation coordination, and sub-registrar filings for all deed types.
